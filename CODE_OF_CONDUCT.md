# Code Of Conduct

RepoPilot AI follows the Contributor Covenant spirit: be respectful, direct, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give feedback that is specific and actionable.
- Focus criticism on ideas, code, behavior, and outcomes.
- Help keep the project safe for contributors and users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Demeaning language or repeated disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Attempts to introduce malicious code, exfiltrate secrets, or bypass project safety gates.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns or security-sensitive abuse privately through the process in `SECURITY.md`.
